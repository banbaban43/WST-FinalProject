Project One

ISurvivor.

iSurvivor is a Vampire Survivor inspired game where all you have to do is survive the hordes of slimes. Pick up Powerups to keep up to the ever-evolving slimes.



Controls

WASD - To Move

SPACE - To Dash

LMB - To Shoot

Q - To Toggle Auto-Attack
